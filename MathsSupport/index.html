Team Maths: Support Page

Team Maths has created a resource room in H6. It is open to all students every Monday and Thursday, from 1:10pm to 1:45pm. This page provides contact details of teachers and students whom you can contact via email. Each person has a short paragraph describing their area of expertise. Simply select the person who you think would best be able to answer your question and click the link to send an email.
